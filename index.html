3in1 WheelChair

Hand Gestures Wheelchair Controller boasts three distinct modes, each tailored to cater to different user preferences and scenarios. The first mode, automatic, utilizes ultrasonic sensors to detect obstacles in the wheelchair's path, ensuring seamless navigation and safety for the user. The second mode offers traditional remote control functionality, providing users with manual control over the wheelchair's movements.
However, what truly sets our project apart is the third mode: hand gestures control. Leveraging cutting-edge technology including LH bridge, Arduino Nano, DC motors, and HS-05 sensor for Bluetooth connectivity, users can intuitively control the wheelchair using simple hand gestures. This revolutionary approach not only enhances user experience but also opens up new possibilities for individuals with limited mobility to navigate their surroundings with ease and independence.
